This is my favorite cat type, a siames cat. I like all cats but the siames have my favorite pelt color of all of them.

This is one of my favorite lizards. Horned chameleons are just like regular chameleons, but with horns.

Lionesses are one of my favorite big cats. You may be wondering why I didn't just grab any old picture of a lion, I personally find lionesses superior to lions. They're majestic, strong and they do basically all of the work for the whole pride

Ravens are my favorite bird because they are incredibly smart and quirky, they act pretty much just like humans (visiting their aging parents, playing around with eachother etc.) and I find it quite incredible
